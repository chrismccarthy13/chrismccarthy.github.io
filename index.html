About Me

Hello, I'm Chris McCarthy, a new full stack developer to the San Diego area.

I am currently a software developer in training at LEARN Academy, which is an innovative full stack web development boot camp. I am passionate about being able to create projects from scratch and solving problems.

In my most recent experiences, Ive had the opportunity to serve in the U.S. Navy and obtain my bachelors degree in financial management. Within my time in the Navy, I learned invaluable skills such as the importance of communication, the ability to work on a team, and staying focused on the mission objective. While I enjoyed my time in the service and studying finance, I realized how enthusiastic I was about computer science after one of my professors did a lecture on blockchain technology and crypto currency. Since then, I have been fascinated with the world of web development and the combination of finance and technology.

I seek to leverage these skills in a career as a software developer working for a financial tech company or a startup as a front end web developer.
